## Query 2: Readings in a date range with demand_mw > 45000

Filters for high-demand hours during the July 1-7, 2018 heat wave.

```python
collection.find({
    "timestamp": {"$gte": datetime(2018, 7, 1), "$lte": datetime(2018, 7, 7)},
    "demand_mw": {"$gt": 45000},
}).sort("timestamp", 1)
```
